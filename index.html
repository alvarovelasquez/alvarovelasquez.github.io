About

In my primary capacity as the leader of the $30M+ Machine Intelligence sub-portfolio of investments for the Information Directorate of the Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL/RI), I provide technical oversight and guidance to existing programs in the area of Artificial Intelligence and, in particular, Machine Learning. I also propose new directions of research and programmatic investments for the lab to undertake as part of the yearly investment strategy. In my secondary capacity as a researcher, I investigate novel solutions in the areas of assured and efficient autonomous systems. This collaborative effort includes the mentoring and funding of students and faculty.
